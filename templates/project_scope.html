FeWork

An Online Portal for Providing Freelancer Work to Housewives

The project "Online Portal for Providing Freelancer Work to Housewives" encompasses several key areas to ensure its effectiveness and success in connecting housewives with freelance work opportunities. The scope of this project includes:

  • Platform Development
  • User (Housewives and Companies) Registration and Profiles
  • Job Posting and Browsing
  • Ratings and Reviews
  • Mobile Accessibility
  • Community Building

The primary objectives of the "Online Portal for Providing Freelancer Work to Housewives" project are outlined as follows:

  • Empower Housewives through Employment
  • Build a Supportive Community
  • Ensure Accessibility
  • Flexible Work Opportunities
  • Skill Utilization and Enhancement
  • Flexible Work Opportunities
  • Economic Independence
